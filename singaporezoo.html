SINGAPORE ZOO

Home to more than 2,400 specimens of over 300 species!
Get ready for a wild adventure with over 2,800 animals inhabiting our lush tropical rainforest.


THINGS TO DO

  • Wildlife Tours

    • Get a "backstage pass" and let our guide drive you around VIP-style in a buggy to some of our most popular exhibits. Then, take a behind-the-scenes look at how we care for our animals!

    Private Tours

    Private Buggy Tour:

    Up to 5 pax

    Embark on a free and easy tour with your personal guide on your own private buggy.

    Let us plan out your itinerary from the exhibits and shows you would like to visit as we share fun facts about our animals!

    Enjoy the VIP treatment with reserved show seating and priority park admission.



    Behind-the-scenes tours

    Reptopia Tour:

    Up to 5 pax

    Learn about the amazing adaptations of Reptiles and the threats they are facing.

    Observe close-up feeding sessions of our scaly friends with plenty of photo opportunities!



  • Feed the Animals

    • Feel the excitement as the animals eat out of your hand.

    - Elephant Feeding

    - Giraffe Feeding

    - Goat Feeding

    - White Rhinoceros Feeding

    - Giant Tortoise




  • Shows

    • Here at this family-friendly zoo, we have:

    - Splash Safari Show for kids splish 'n' splash!

    - Animal Friends Show for interaction with animals!

    - Rainforest Fights Back Show featuring a diverse range of rainforest animals!

    - Elephant Presentation (Temporarily suspended)


    ... and many more!
